ABOUT ME

Hello! I am a UC Berkeley undergraduate studying physics and computer science. I am most excited by projects that connect these disciplines in creative ways to produce new functionalities, modalities, and theories! As such, I am fascinated by optics, computational imaging, and computer graphics -- fields where interdisciplinary projects of this flavor are ubiquitous. More generally, I adore learning -- particularly if the topic is highly visual, geometric, or mathematical. Thanks for visiting my webpage! I hope you find some of the featured work interesting!
